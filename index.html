What is MortalCoin? ü•∑

MortalCoin is a Web3 game designed as a Telegram Mini App. It combines elements of gaming and cryptocurrency trading, offering a unique battle experience where players fight using real cryptocurrencies as their fighters.

Key Features

Real-Time Crypto Battles

The outcome of each battle is determined by live market performance.

Trade-Driven Gameplay

Players strategically select cryptocurrencies as fighters, leveraging market movements to win.

Ladder Mode

A survival challenge inspired by Mortal Kombat's tower system.

How Does the Battles Work?

Each match is a short duel between two cryptocurrencies, where the coin with the best price performance over a set timeframe (currently 10 seconds) wins. The battle system is powered by real-time crypto market data, ensuring fair and dynamic fights.
